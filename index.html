Heres how to write a Resume:

1. Provide your contact information

Listing this information at the top of your resume gives hiring managers an easy way to contact you in case they want to request further information or set up an interview.

2. Create an objective statement

After your contact information, include an objective statement that shows your relevant skills, work experience and professional goals in one to two sentences.

3. Include work experience

List any previous work experience you have, highlighting work that relates to the nonprofit sector. Be sure to list any volunteer work or internships you ve completed. Provide the name of the organization, your job title, the duration of your employment and the specific duties you held while working there
